The story of Memori

Memori is a team that preserves what matters most in life — your moments. We shoot professional photo and video, create custom clips and slide shows, print photos, and make sure no important event gets lost in time.

Our videographers and photographers do everything to make your moment unforgettable. We put not only technology but also soul into every project so images convey genuine emotions.

Our goal is simple — to give people the chance to return to their memories whenever they wish. A wedding, a birthday, a baby’s first smile, a concert, a performance, or just an ordinary day that became special — all this can be relived by opening an album or pressing play.

We do this because we believe moments are true wealth. Things can be replaced, but feelings, emotions, and memories are priceless. That’s why Memori was created — to stop time in a frame and give people a memory that does not fade.

Our philosophy is simple: life is made of moments — and we make them timeless.
